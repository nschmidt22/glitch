About

My school offers many enrichments available for students to take, as all students are required to have completed at least 6 enrichments by the end of their senior year. While it is possible to do independent and self-designed enrichments, this activity is one of the official ones provided by the school. It's purpose is to introduce students to HTML, CSS, and general concepts of building a website from scratch. Despite having already learned some HTML and CSS in the past, I had been focusing on other languages and wanted to make sure I remembered how to create websites.

Concepts covered

Basic vocabulary (ie: HTML, web, router, Telnet, domain, etc.), general formatting, types of elements on a webpage, linking, spacing and margins, changing characteristics of certain elements, managing imported images, CSS, and an intro to javascript integration.
