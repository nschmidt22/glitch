Throughout all of middle school, I participated in Mathcounts, where students would go to learn about math concepts that either weren't covered in school or made us think about certain math problems with a different perspective. Every year, there would also be a math competition that students who volunteered and passed the volunteer test (or were among the top few scores) would attend.

My Participation

I attended after school and after school sports from 4 to 5pm. Many of my friends also participated in Mathcounts, so we tended to collaborate on group problems and enjoy ourselves more than if we'd been alone.
