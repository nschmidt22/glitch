When did I play soccer?

I participated in my school's soccer team from kindergarten to 2nd grade. We were called the Emerson Eagles, and it was an all girls team. I would have continued to play, but my family and I moved the summer between 2nd and 3rd grade, so I switched schools.

My Role on the Team

I usually played as either the goalie or defense, but I'm fairly sure I've played every position on the field at some point or another. Since we were quite young, no one really specialized in certain positions, but some of us frequented certain spots more than others.
