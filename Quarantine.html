About

The Quarantine Online Science Tournament was hosted by the Indiana High School Science Championship on the weekend of July 31 - August 2. It was meant to encourage the continued participation in STEM-related activities throughout quarantine, and was brought up to me through my school's Science Olympiad team.

My Participation

My events were astronomy, inorganic chemistry, and computer science. I had surprisingly been fairly busy around the time that it happened, so I didn't have much time to prepare, but we did alright overall. I think that this event reminded me that despite how much I've learned about these topics, I still have a lot that I have yet to master.
